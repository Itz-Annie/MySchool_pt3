Fairtrade Award
we are a FairAware School!!!!!!

Crookfur marked Fairtrade Fortnight with pupils from P5/6, P6A and P6B sharing their learning at assembly. These classes have been learning about Fairtrade this term and told us all about different Fairtrade products, how Fairtrade benefits farmers and workers, and what we can do to help support Fairtrade. As a result of their excellent work, Crookfur has achieved the FairAware Award which we hope to build on in the future.


Glasgow Science Festival: Cluster K’nex Challenge
we are a FairAware School!!!!!!

Two of our Primary 6 pupils who competed in the Glasgow Science Festival: Cluster K’nex Challenge. They had visited Eastwood High School to compete against other schools in our cluster. This challenge involved working together to build a theme park ride in a one hour time frame. The ride had to be 30cm tall, have six seats and be able to move which the team managed to achieve by making a Ferris wheel. They received some excellent feedback from the judges including the fact they communicated well throughout the whole task and overcame any challenges that they faced.
